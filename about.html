Our Story

Anubis Underground, a Slovenian organization based in Ljubljana, is not just an event organizer but a community of people who go beyond the commercial rhythms of music and instead embrace solid underground sounds.

Our Sound

Although the boundaries between genres are slightly blurred, we play and listen to what feels right — music that resonates with the collective as well as with true ravers, creating an unforgettable atmosphere.

Our Beginning

We began with the idea of giving young people a place to rave during the pandemic, believing that mental health is what matters most in such times.

Our Mission

Now that the collective no longer needs to hide, our main goal is to create a space for the young — and the young at heart — where they can disconnect and dance until the early morning hours.
